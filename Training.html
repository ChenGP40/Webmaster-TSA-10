Included with your purchase of your ticket is our one-month-long training course. THIS IS REQUIRED IF YOU WANT TO GO INTO SPACE. You will learn how to be an astronaut all in the span of a month. This course is grueling because there is a lot of information you will need to learn in a very short timeframe.
What you will be trained for the following:

  • Withstanding the incredible weight of gravity forced upon you during launch.
  • Putting on a spacesuit.
  • Dealing with weightlessness in space, which includes:
  • - Movement.
  • - Using the restroom.
  • - Sleeping.
  • - Eating.

After this course, you will be familiar with all of the above and more.
