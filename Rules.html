In order for you to get on the ship to experience this amazing journey into outer space, there are a few requirements you must meet.
Please read both sections carefully.
BASIC REQUIREMENTS
  • You must be above the age of 21 years old AND below the age of 70 years old.
  • You may not board if you are pregnant.
  • Your criminal record must be clear from any serious crimes or felonies.
BANNED ITEMS
  • Weapons of any kind
  • Any non-approved medication or drugs
  • Alcohol
  • Ammunition
  • Batteries
  • Cigarettes
  • E-cigarettes
  • Explosives
  • Lighters
  • Any other item our security team rejects
