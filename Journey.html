Found on this page is the route you'll take if you decide on traveling with us!
Phase 1: Earth
Step OneFill out your application. Be sure to fill out everything and read the contracts before signing!
Step Two
Go through training at the Johnson Space Center. This includes adapting to both the high G's of launch and the 0 G's of space travel.
Step Three
Travel to the Horizon launch pad located at the Guiana Space Centre in Kourou, French Guiana.
Step Four
Launch at the designated launch time.
Phase 2: SPACE
Step Five
Sit back, relax, and enjoy the ride!
Phase 3: Return
Step 6
Land on Earth at the Horizon entry pad (also located at the Guiana Space Centre).
Step 7
Exit the complex and return home!
